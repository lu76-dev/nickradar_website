Two Distinct User Groups

nickradar processes data for two separate groups: Event Admins (B2B customers who purchase sticker packages and manage events) and Participants (guests who use the app during an event). Different data is collected and retained for each group.

Data Collected — Event Admins

  • Organization name, billing address, VAT number (for invoicing)
  • Contact person name and phone number
  • Email address and password (hashed)
  • Payment data processed exclusively via Stripe — nickradar does not store full card details
  • Invoice and order history

Data Collected — Participants

  • 4-digit entry code (linked to a nickname and event)
  • Optional profile photo and slogan
  • Session token and IP address (for session management only)
  • Chat messages (deleted immediately after event ends)
  • Connection requests and responses (deleted immediately after event ends)

Participants do not register with an email address or password. No personal account exists beyond the event session.

Data Retention (GDPR Art. 5)

  • Participant messages, sessions, codes: deleted immediately after event ends
  • Participant profile photos and slogans: deleted immediately after event ends
  • Event Admin account data: until account deletion
  • Billing and invoice data: 7 years (Austrian tax law, BAO §132)
  • Abuse reports: retained as long as necessary for handling

Legal Basis

Event Admin data: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (performance of contract) and Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR (legal obligation for invoicing).
Participant data: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (performance of contract — event session) and Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in platform security).

Data Sharing

nickradar does not share personal data with third parties, except where required by law. Stripe processes payment data under their own privacy policy: stripe.com/privacy. In cases involving criminal offences, data may be disclosed upon official request.

Your Rights

  • Access (Art. 15), Rectification (Art. 16), Erasure (Art. 17)
  • Restriction of processing (Art. 18), Objection (Art. 21 GDPR)
  • Data portability (Art. 20 GDPR)

Requests: info@nickradar.com

Analytics

This website uses Umami Analytics — a privacy-friendly, cookie-free analytics tool hosted on our own server. No personal data is transmitted to third parties.

Chat Privacy

Chat messages between participants are not accessible to anyone outside the conversation. Neither nickradar, nor any of its staff, nor Event Admins, promoters, or cooperation partners have access to chat content. Messages are stored temporarily on nickradar servers solely for the purpose of delivering them between participants during the event, and are permanently and automatically deleted immediately after the event ends.

Data Security

All connections are encrypted via HTTPS. Passwords are hashed with bcrypt. Sessions use secure random tokens. Participant data is automatically and permanently deleted at event end.
