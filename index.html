Two Distinct User Groups

nickradar processes data for two separate groups: Event Admins (B2B customers who purchase sticker packages and manage events) and Participants (guests who use the app during an event). Different data is collected and retained for each group.

Data Collected — Event Admins

  • Organization name, billing address, VAT number (for invoicing)
  • Contact person name and phone number
  • Email address and password (hashed)
  • Payment data processed exclusively via Stripe — nickradar does not store full card details
  • Invoice and order history

Data Collected — Participants

  • 4-digit entry code (linked to a nickname and event)
  • Optional profile photo and slogan
  • Session token and IP address (for session management only)
  • Chat messages (deleted immediately after event ends)
  • Connection requests and responses (deleted immediately after event ends)

Participants do not register with an email address or password. No personal account exists beyond the event session.

Data Retention (GDPR Art. 5)

  • Participant messages, sessions, codes: deleted immediately after event ends
  • Participant profile photos and slogans: deleted immediately after event ends
  • Event Admin account data: until account deletion
  • Billing and invoice data: 7 years (Austrian tax law, BAO §132)
  • Abuse reports: retained as long as necessary for handling

Legal Basis

Event Admin data: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (performance of contract) and Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR (legal obligation for invoicing).
Participant data: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (performance of contract — event session) and Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in platform security).

Data Sharing

nickradar does not share personal data with third parties, except where required by law. Stripe processes payment data under their own privacy policy: stripe.com/privacy. In cases involving criminal offences, data may be disclosed upon official request.

Chat Privacy

Chat messages between participants are not accessible to anyone outside the conversation. Neither nickradar, nor any of its staff, nor Event Admins, promoters, or cooperation partners have access to chat content. Messages are stored temporarily on nickradar servers solely for the purpose of delivering them between participants during the event, and are permanently and automatically deleted immediately after the event ends.

Data Security

All connections are encrypted via HTTPS. Passwords are hashed with bcrypt. Sessions use secure random tokens. Participant data is automatically and permanently deleted at event end.

§ 2 The Service

nickradar provides a digital connecting tool for live events. Event Admins purchase nickname sticker packages, distribute them to their guests on-site, and enable guests to connect with each other anonymously during the event via app.nickradar.com. nickradar provides technical infrastructure only and is not involved in the organization or execution of events.

§ 3 Registration and Account

  • Registration requires a valid organization name, billing address, and email address
  • Accounts are activated manually by nickradar — there is no automatic activation
  • Account credentials must be kept confidential and must not be shared with third parties
  • One account per organization — multiple accounts for the same organization are not permitted
  • nickradar reserves the right to decline or deactivate registrations without stating reasons

§ 4 Ordering Sticker Packages

  • Sticker packages can be ordered via the Event Admin Portal at events.nickradar.com
  • Minimum order: 24 stickers per event
  • Additional stickers can be purchased at any time before or during an active event (minimum 5)
  • After successful payment, nicknames and codes are generated immediately and irrevocably
  • The Event Admin is responsible for printing stickers on self-adhesive label paper (standard A4)

§ 5 Pricing and Payment

  • Pricing is tiered based on quantity — the more stickers, the lower the unit price
  • All prices are in EUR and exclusive of VAT where applicable
  • Payment is due in advance before event activation
  • Payment is processed via Stripe (credit card) or manually confirmed by nickradar
  • nickradar reserves the right to adjust prices. Changes will be communicated in advance and do not affect already placed orders.

§ 6 No Refund Policy

Once a sticker package has been generated (nicknames and codes assigned), the order is final and non-refundable. This applies regardless of whether all stickers were activated or the event was stopped early. By placing an order, the Event Admin explicitly acknowledges and accepts this policy.

§ 7 Event Activation

An event becomes active when payment has been confirmed by nickradar. The event starts automatically at the scheduled time and ends after 8 hours, unless stopped manually by the Event Admin or nickradar.

§ 8 Responsibilities of the Event Admin

  • The Event Admin is fully responsible for the organization and execution of their event
  • The Event Admin is responsible for ensuring that participation is voluntary and age-appropriate (minimum age 18)
  • The Event Admin is the first point of contact for any participant reports and must respond promptly on-site
  • If a report is not addressed within 15 minutes, nickradar may intervene directly
  • The Event Admin must not collect participant data on behalf of nickradar
  • The Event Admin must inform participants about the nickradar system before distributing stickers

§ 9 Data Protection

Participant data (messages, sessions, codes) is permanently deleted from nickradar servers immediately after the event ends. Event Admins are responsible for compliance with data protection obligations on their own side.
