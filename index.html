§ 4 Contacting Rules

  • Requests remain active for 12 hours
  • A mutual YES opens a 48-hour chat
  • NO: permanent mutual block — no further interactions possible
  • EXPIRED (no reply): no cost — both parties may send new requests
  • No reply does not count as rejection
  • Chat messages and location data are permanently deleted from our servers immediately after the chat ends — regardless of the reason (expired, denied, blocked or finished)
