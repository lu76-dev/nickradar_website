Participant Terms

This is a digital connecting tool for live events. You received a sticker with a nickname and a 4-digit code. By entering your code at app.nickradar.com you join the event as a Participant and can connect anonymously with other participants on-site.

Direct Contact

By joining this event you understand that other visible participants at the same event may contact you directly through the app. No phone number or email address is ever revealed to other participants. Any chat can be blocked at any time, ending contact immediately and permanently.

Your Data

  • Your session is linked to your sticker code only
  • Optional: you may add a profile photo and a short intro (max. 30 characters)
  • When the event ends, your chat messages, session, and profile are removed from the system — nickradar does not retain this data beyond the event duration. There is no account to log back into.

Your Conduct

You agree to use the platform respectfully. The following is strictly prohibited:

  • Harassment, threats, or intimidation
  • Unsolicited sexual content
  • Stalking or repeated unwanted contact
  • Any illegal activity

You are solely responsible for your messages and behavior on the platform. If you feel unsafe or need assistance, please approach event staff in person immediately. nickradar is not an emergency service and does not provide on-site supervision or security.

Law Enforcement

During an active event, nickradar may disclose available data to competent authorities to the extent required by applicable law, and only upon a valid, legally binding request or order. Such cooperation is strictly limited to the duration of the active event.
