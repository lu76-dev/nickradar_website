What is nickradar?

nickradar is a digital connecting tool for live events. You received a sticker with a nickname and a 4-digit code. By entering your code you join the event as a participant and can connect anonymously with other participants on-site.

Your Data

  • No name, no email address, no phone number is collected
  • Your session is linked to your sticker code only
  • Optional: you may add a profile photo and a short slogan
  • All your data — messages, chats, profile — is permanently and automatically deleted from nickradar servers immediately after the event ends

Your Conduct

By joining the event you agree to use the platform respectfully. The following is strictly prohibited:

  • Harassment, threats, or intimidation
  • Unsolicited sexual content
  • Stalking or repeated unwanted contact
  • Any illegal activity

You are responsible for your own messages and behavior on the platform.

Law Enforcement

nickradar does not store chat content after the event ends. During an active event, nickradar may cooperate with competent authorities in the context of criminal investigations, subject to a valid court order. Such cooperation is strictly limited to the duration of the active event.

Blocking & Anonymity

  • You can block any chat at any time — contact ends immediately and permanently
  • No phone number or email address is ever revealed to other participants
  • Requests can be ignored — no reply means no contact

Event End

When the event ends, your session is invalidated and all data is deleted. There is no account to log back into.
